What HireTrue checks

Three modules, built for India

📋

JD Auditor

Scores job descriptions on bias, clarity, candidate appeal, and realism. Flags gender-coded language, exclusionary phrases, and inflated requirements common in Indian hiring.

  • Bias & gender language detection
  • IIT/NIT elitism flags
  • Candidate appeal score
  • Line-by-line rewrite suggestions
Most Used
📄

Offer Decoder

Decodes offer letters into plain English. Surfaces red-flag clauses, unenforceable non-competes, asymmetric notice periods, and negotiation opportunities.

  • IP & non-compete clause risk
  • Notice period asymmetry
  • Variable pay structure analysis
  • India industry benchmarking
⚖️

Policy Gap Finder

Checks HR policies against Indian Labour Codes (2019–2020), POSH Act 2013, Maternity Benefit Act, and more. Gives a compliance score with specific law references.

  • POSH Act & ICC compliance
  • Labour Code 2020 gaps
  • Maternity & gratuity checks
  • Missing clause identification
