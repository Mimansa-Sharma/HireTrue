About

Built by Mimansa Sharma
for India's HR reality

Indian job seekers sign documents they do not fully understand. HR teams post job descriptions that quietly filter out strong candidates. Resumes get rejected by automated systems before a human ever sees them. LensHR was built to fix that.

Most HR tools are designed for Western markets and are disconnected from India's labour law landscape. LensHR is built from the ground up for how hiring and employment actually work in India.
