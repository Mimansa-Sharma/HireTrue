What LensHR checks

Three modules, built for India

JD

JD Auditor

Scores job descriptions on bias, clarity, candidate appeal, and realism. Flags exclusionary language and inflated requirements common in Indian hiring.

  • Bias and gender language detection
  • Candidate appeal score
  • Realism check on requirements
  • Line-by-line rewrite suggestions
Most Used
OL

Offer Decoder

Translates offer letters into plain language. Surfaces risky clauses, one-sided terms, and negotiation opportunities specific to Indian employment context.

  • IP and non-compete clause risk
  • Notice period analysis
  • Variable pay structure breakdown
  • India industry benchmarking
PG

Policy Gap Finder

Checks HR policies against India's four consolidated Labour Codes (November 2025), which unified 29 legacy laws into a single framework.

  • Consolidated Labour Code compliance
  • Statutory leave entitlement checks
  • Grievance mechanism gaps
  • Missing clause identification

About

Built by Mimansa Sharma
for India's HR reality

Indian job seekers sign documents they do not fully understand. HR teams post job descriptions that quietly filter out strong candidates. Policies go unchecked against laws that have changed. LensHR was built to fix that.

Most HR tools are designed for Western markets and are disconnected from India's labour law landscape. LensHR is built from the ground up for how hiring and employment actually work in India.

Where most tools give general feedback, LensHR identifies the exact problem, scores it, and tells you what to do next. It works for both sides of the table — HR professionals who write and review documents, and individuals who need to understand what they are being asked to accept.

India-first

Built around India's consolidated Labour Codes and local hiring norms, not borrowed frameworks from other markets.

Scored, not vague

Every analysis delivers real scores for bias, compliance, and appeal so you know exactly where you stand and what to address first.

Both sides of the table

Built equally for HR professionals managing documents and for individuals trying to understand what they are signing.
